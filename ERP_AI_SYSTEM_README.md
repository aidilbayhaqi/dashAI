# 🚀 ERP AI System

Sistem Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) berbasis web dengan integrasi AI Agent untuk business intelligence dan decision support system.

---

## 📌 Overview

ERP AI System adalah platform manajemen perusahaan terpusat yang mencakup:

- Finance Management
- HRD & KPI Performance System
- CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
- Product Management
- Reporting & Analytics
- AI Agent Decision System

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

- Integrasi seluruh aktivitas bisnis perusahaan
- Analisis keuangan real-time
- KPI karyawan berbasis data
- CRM pipeline management
- Product management terpusat
- AI Agent untuk decision support

---

### Company & Branch
